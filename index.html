Aries (March 21 - April 19)


The first sign of the zodiac, Aries loves to be number one. Naturally, this dynamic fire sign is no stranger to competition. Bold and ambitious, Aries dives headfirst into even the most challenging situations and they'll make sure they always come out on top.

Taurus (April 20 - May 20)


Taurus is the sign most likely to indulge in a six-hour bath, a Swedish massage, and a decadent dessert spread. As an earth sign, they enjoy relaxing in peaceful, sensory-rich environments with soothing sounds, aromas, and delicious flavors.

Gemini (May 21 - June 20)


Gemini is the sign that wishes for a clone to handle all their interests. Spontaneous, playful, and curious, they’re driven by a constant need to explore, symbolized by the celestial twins.

Cancer (June 21 - July 22)


Cancer, represented by the crab, balances emotional and material worlds with ease. Highly intuitive, their psychic abilities manifest in real-life situations. Like their shell, Cancers protect their emotions fiercely, and earning their trust is key to truly knowing them.

Leo (July 23 - August 22)


Leo, represented by the lion, is passionate, loyal, and dramatic. As fiery kings and queens of the zodiac, they thrive in the spotlight, embracing their royal, vivacious nature and love for celebration.

Virgo (August 23 - September 22)


Virgo, the logical and practical earth sign, is known for their systematic approach to life. Represented by the goddess of wheat, they thrive in the material world, constantly perfecting skills through diligent practice and a focus on detail.

Libra (September 23 - October 22)


Libra is defined by balance, harmony, and justice. Represented by the scales, this cardinal air sign seeks equilibrium in all aspects of life, especially in matters of the heart.

Scorpio (October 23 - November 21)


Scorpio is mysterious and often misunderstood. As a water sign, they harness emotional energy and psychic abilities, gaining wisdom and courage from both the seen and unseen realms. This makes them one of the most dynamic and complex signs.

Sagittarius (November 22 - December 21)


Sagittarius, represented by the archer, is a boundless seeker of knowledge. As the last fire sign, they pursue geographical, intellectual, and spiritual adventures with the energy of blazing arrows.

Capricorn (December 22 - January 19)


For Capricorn, time is the most valuable resource. Represented by the sea goat, they climb steadily to the top with patience, perseverance, and dedication, skillfully navigating both material and emotional realms.

Aquarius (January 20 - February 18)


Aquarius, the last air sign, is innovative, progressive, and revolutionary. Represented by the water bearer, they are deeply humanitarian and dedicated to making the world a better place.

Pisces (February 19 - March 20)


Pisces, the most intuitive and empathetic sign, absorbs lessons from all the other signs. Represented by two fish swimming in opposite directions, they balance between fantasy and reality, embodying deep sensitivity and insight.
